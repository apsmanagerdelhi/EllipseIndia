Our Vision

We envision a rural population composed mostly of proud and successful entrepreneurs. Ellipse Enterprise   wants to become the leading catalysts and business model provider for Rural to become highly motivated as well as effective entrepreneurs. Business Innovation through Social Inclusion.

Our Mission

Our mission requires the highest ideals and dedication. Atulya Ventures desires to Convert every rural into a professional and effective entrepreneur. To be an innovator And implementer for increasing growth in new businesses which are uncharted in India. Innovating and putting our best efforts to connect Rural and Urban Services and Products is the theme around which our business growth is defined.

Our Goals

Today the Corporate Governance of Government Of India has a steep challenge of Clean Governance with need to bridge the Urban - Rural divide with a sustainable and repeatable model basis of which multiple basic services can be made available to the rural population along with Skills such that the Rural people do not feel the need to relocate to Urban areas and live in inhuman conditions. Our Objective is to eliminate this divide and evangelize an AURUM framework which can be replicated in all states of our country embodying the Indian Culture of in the Rural and Urban Areas too.
